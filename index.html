HOW IT WORKS

Usage Instructions:

  1. Apply a small amount of melanQ to slightly damp hair after dewing.
  2. Comb through to ensure even distribution.
  3. Allow your hair to dry naturally or use a hairdryer.
  4. For optimal results, expose your hair to direct sunlight after application.

At the beginning of use, apply melanQ 3–4 times per week, preferably in the morning. Once your hair begins to return to its original color (within a week), reduce application to 1–2 times per week as needed.
