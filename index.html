How It Works

  1. Apply a small amount of MelonQ to slightly damp hair after dewing.
  2. Comb through to ensure even distribution.
  3. Allow your hair to dry naturally or use a hairdryer.
  4. Expose your hair to direct sunlight after application.

At the beginning of use, apply MelonQ 3–4 times per week, preferably in the morning. Once your hair begins to return to its original color (within a week), reduce application to 1–2 times per week as needed.
